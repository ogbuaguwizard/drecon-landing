Meet Sulaiman Abu

With over 17 years of dynamic, hands-on experience that spans the full spectrum of the real estate value chain in Nigeria, I've built a reputation as a transformative figure in Nigeria's rapidly evolving property landscape. Born and raised in Somolu, Lagos, I hold a Higher National Diploma (HND) in Estate Management from Federal Polytechnic, Auchi, Edo State. My journey in real estate began from the ground up, acquiring practical knowledge through sheer dedication and perseverance. My robust skill set encompasses financial projections, design creation, project conceptualization, and strategic management that have consistently set me apart in the industry.
