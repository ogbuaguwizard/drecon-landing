Meet Tunde Adebayo

Founder & CEO of DRECON

With over 20 years of experience in urban development and infrastructure projects, Tunde Adebayo is a visionary leader dedicated to transforming Nigeria's landscape through sustainable development.

Under his leadership, DRECON has completed over 150 projects across 24 states, impacting millions of Nigerians through improved infrastructure, community development, and economic opportunities.

"Our mission at DRECON is simple: to build a Nigeria where every community has access to modern infrastructure, economic opportunities, and sustainable living environments. We believe that development should empower people and create lasting growth."

Tunde Adebayo
Founder & Chief Executive Officer
